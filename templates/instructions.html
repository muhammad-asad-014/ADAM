How to use ADAM

For Teachers

  • Access the Teacher Portal to manage quiz, create quiz, and view detailed quiz reports.
  • Quizzes are created using a JSON structure, allowing for complex question types and scoring.
  • Generated reports provide deep insight into student performance, timing, and academic integrity flags.

For Students (Assessment Rules)

  • You will be timed per question, not for the whole quiz. Pay close attention to the countdown timer.
  • Once an option is selected, your answer is final and cannot be changed.
  • If the timer expires, the question is automatically marked as unanswered/incorrect, and you proceed to the next one.

ACADEMIC INTEGRITY WARNING

The quiz environment is secured. Do not switch tabs, minimize the window, or attempt to navigate away while an assessment is active.

INSTANT PENALTY: Any unauthorized navigation will result in the immediate expiration of the current question's timer.

Technical Requirements

  • Use a modern, updated browser (Chrome, Firefox, Edge) for the best experience.
  • A highly stable internet connection is mandatory.
